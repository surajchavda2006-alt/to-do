Help & FAQ

How do I add a task?

Go to the To-Do List page, type your task in the input box, and click "Add".

How do I mark a task as completed?

Click on the task text to toggle its completed status.

Can I delete tasks?

Yes, click the "Delete" button next to the task you want to remove.

Where are my tasks saved?

Your tasks are saved locally in your browser's localStorage. They will persist on the same device and browser.
